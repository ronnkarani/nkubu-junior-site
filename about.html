About Nkubu Junior Academy

Headteacher's Message

Welcome to Nkubu Junior Academy! As the headteacher, I take great pride in leading a school that is passionate about nurturing young minds. At Nkubu Junior Academy, we believe in providing not just academic excellence, but also in instilling discipline, compassion, and confidence in every learner.

We thank all our stakeholders—parents, teachers, and students—for building a strong learning community together.

– Mrs. Jane Muthoni
Headteacher

Our School

Nkubu Junior Academy is a premier primary school located in the heart of Meru County, dedicated to nurturing excellence in young learners. We offer a holistic learning environment grounded in integrity, discipline, and creativity.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers every child to reach their full potential in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that shapes future leaders through academic excellence and strong moral values.
