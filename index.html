Our Story

Founded in 2010, Nkubu Junior Academy has grown into a nurturing center of excellence, focused on developing holistic, value-driven learners who are well-prepared for the future. Nkubu Junior Academy began with a vision to create a safe and inspiring learning environment where every child is empowered to thrive. We focus on both academic excellence and nurturing core values such as responsibility, empathy, and innovation.

Through partnerships with families and the community, we guide students on a journey of personal and intellectual growth.
