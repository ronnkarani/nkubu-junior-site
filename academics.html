Academics

Our Academic Approach

Nkubu Junior Academy is committed to offering a balanced and holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and physical development of each child. We follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), ensuring learners acquire real-life skills alongside academic knowledge.

Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2)

We offer a nurturing environment for early learners with emphasis on play-based and exploratory learning.

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

Foundation building in numeracy, literacy, and life skills with personalized learning support.

Upper Primary (Grades 4–6)

Competency development through a well-rounded academic and co-curricular program to prepare for junior secondary.
