Admissions

Join Our Learning Community

Thank you for your interest in Nkubu Junior Academy. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and are committed to making the admission process smooth and informative for parents and guardians.

Pre-Primary

  • Age 4–5 years
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Health/immunization records

Primary (Grades 1–6)

  • Age 6+ years
  • Previous school report (if any)
  • Copy of birth certificate

Transfer Students

  • Letter of transfer
  • Most recent report card
  • Interview with headteacher

How to Apply

  1. Download and fill the application form.
  2. Attach required documents (birth certificate, reports, etc.).
  3. Submit in person or email to: admissions@nkubujunioracademy.ac.ke
  4. Wait for an interview invitation.
